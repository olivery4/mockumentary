Mercury properties

  1. Slivery-white
  2. Liquid metal

Mercury Uses

  1. Scientific Instruments
  2. Electrical appliances
  3. Chemical industry
    • Used as catalyst in various chemical processes
  4. Used in dental fillings
  5. Pesticides
  6. Anti-fouling paint
  7. Batteries
  8. Skin-whitening

Where to find mercury

  • Rocks and soil
  • Can be released through natural means:
    • Volcanoes
    • Hot springs near mercury ore
  • Mining
  • Fossil fuels
  • Industrial processes (which releases significant amounts of mercury)
